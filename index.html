⚙️ FEATURES / 核心功能

🎨

TEXT TO IMAGE / 文本到图像

Nano Banana model generates 518x518 images from text prompts in just 1.8 seconds. 模型从文本提示生成图像。

🧱

IMAGE TO 3D / 图像到3D

Hierarchical 3D generation with 8192-dim latent space and DINOv2 ViT-g/14 encoder creates detailed meshes. 生成精细的3D模型。

🎭

AI MATERIALS / AI自动选材

Gemini 2.5 Flash vision model intelligently assigns Minecraft block materials with 92.3% accuracy. 智能分配MC方块材料。

🔄

REAL-TIME PREVIEW / 实时预览

Interactive Three.js viewer with part-level editing and multi-view rendering for instant feedback. 可交互的3D预览。

📦

SCHEMATIC EXPORT / 蓝图导出

Direct conversion to Minecraft 1.12 .schematic format with NBT encoding and GZIP compression. 直接导出为.schematic格式。

💾

SAVE/LOAD SYSTEM / 保存与加载

Persistent state management with localStorage and ZIP export for session recovery. 支持本地保存和会话恢复。
